TIRUPATI DARSHAN INFORMATION

=== TYPES OF DARSHAN ===

1. Dharma Darshan (Free Darshan)
   - Free of cost for all pilgrims
   - Waiting time: 4 to 12 hours depending on crowd
   - Our package facilitates Dharma Darshan with guide assistance
   - Available every day

2. Special Entry Darshan (SED) - Rs. 300
   - Costs Rs. 300 per person
   - Waiting time: 1 to 4 hours
   - Available via TTD official portal: tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in
   - Slots available 3 months in advance

3. VIP Break Darshan
   - For Indian nationals only
   - Not available under our package's tourist quota
   - Must be arranged through TTD portal directly
   - VIP darshan is NOT guaranteed and NOT part of our packages

4. Divya Darshan (Disabled/Senior)
   - Senior citizen priority is NOT available under our package
   - Separate TTD guidelines apply

=== DARSHAN TIMINGS ===
Temple opens: 2:30 AM
Temple closes: 1:00 AM (next day)
Darshan timings depend heavily on TTD crowd conditions
Generally takes 1 to 5 hours from entry gate to darshan
Our guide will assess the situation and guide accordingly

=== WHAT TO CARRY FOR DARSHAN ===
Valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Driving Licence, Passport, Voter ID)
Comfortable footwear (removed at temple entrance)
Devotional attire (traditional clothing recommended)
No leather items allowed inside temple
No mobile phones allowed in inner sanctum
Children under 5 years - entry free, no darshan ticket needed
Children under 1 year - free darshan allowed

=== TIRUMALA HILL INFORMATION ===
Tirumala is the hill town where the main temple is located
Rooms at Tirumala are NOT permitted under our package as per TTD guidelines
Pilgrims freshen up at base (Tirupati town) before going uphill
Public restrooms available at Tirumala, guide will assist
After Mundan (head tonsuring), freshen-up rooms not provided separately

=== CROWD & RUSH INFORMATION ===
Peak season: October to February (festive season)
Brahmotsavam festival: extremely heavy rush (6-10 hour wait)
Weekdays: generally less crowded than weekends
Our guide monitors crowd and adjusts plan accordingly
